UNSC

The UNSC (United Nations Space Command) is the military, exploratory, and scientific agency of humanity in the Halo universe.

Key Facts

  • Founded in the 22nd century
  • Primary military force of humanity
  • Famous for the Spartan program and the Master Chief

The United Nations Space Command (UNSC) is the military, exploratory, and scientific agency of humanity in the Halo series. It was originally formed to oversee space colonization and protect Earth's interstellar interests. As tensions rose between Earth and its outer colonies, the UNSC became increasingly militarized, eventually playing a key role in suppressing rebellion and maintaining order.

When humanity encountered the alien alliance known as the Covenant the UNSC became the frontline defense force in the Human-Covenant War.
